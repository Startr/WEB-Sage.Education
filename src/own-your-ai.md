# Own Your AI 

## Keys to Empower

> *We are the AI choice your successors thank you for.*
>
> *We give you the freedom to build* — for everyone.

### We're not like other companies

We've been where you are and know how it feels when your tools are taken from you.

---

## You have seen this story before...

**Flipgrid** was acquired, rebranded, and retired. Every video your students recorded — every reflection, every performance, every relationship built through the platform: orphaned with the shutdown.

**Finale**, the music notation software a generation of teachers built their entire workflow around, was discontinued. Decades of notation files became hostages to a format the world no longer reads.

**Thinglink** rewarded its early evangelists with throttling, then paywalls, then blocked their embeds entirely. The teachers who proved the platform's worth became its leverage points.

### This is not bad luck. 

This is what happens when the software schools depend on is not the school's. This is what happens when you rent.

---

## Sage.Education is released under the<br> **GNU Affero General Public License, version 3**

### What this licence prevents

In plain language, what that means for your school:

- **You can self-host.** The code is yours to run on your own hardware, in your own building, under your own control.
- **You can fork.** If we ever change in a way that no longer serves your school, you take what works and continue without us.
- **You can stop paying us and keep using it forever.** There is no licence expiry, no remote kill-switch, no subscription that, when cancelled, makes the software stop working.
- **The model weights are yours. The data is yours.** We have no legal mechanism to claw any of this back.

These permissions are **irrevocable** for the term of copyright. We cannot lock you out. The licence forbids it.

Marketing pages can promise anything. Licences are enforceable.

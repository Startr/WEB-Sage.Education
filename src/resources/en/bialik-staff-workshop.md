## What Sage.Education is

Sage.Education is two things at once: a **guide** that helps your whole school *read*
AI — what it does, what it doesn't, and how to teach with it — and a **platform the
school owns**, an open-source AI workspace that runs on the school's system, where your
data stays with you and everything is editable. It's a tool you shape, not a black box
you rent, and not a replacement for teachers.

## The four things we offer

1. **Guidance, literacy & curriculum** — we help everyone read AI, with a modular curriculum per audience.
2. **The platform you own** — your own AI workspace: your data, your rules, role-aware views for admins, teachers, and learners.
3. **Build your own AI** — make a classroom tool over a coffee, no code, in a couple of minutes.
4. **The Junior Internship** — students build real, open-source AI by *making*, not just using.
